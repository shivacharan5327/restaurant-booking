Our Story

A place rooted in craft & community

Brûlé began as a small corner roastery with one obsession: the perfect cup. Over six years, we've grown into a full kitchen and gathering space — but the obsession hasn't changed.

Every bean is sourced directly from farmers. Every dish uses produce from within 100 km. We believe great food and coffee don't need to travel far to be extraordinary.
